About APPEC College

Building Tomorrow's Professionals Today

Our Mission

To empower students with practical, hands-on education that bridges the gap between learning and real-world application. We are committed to producing skilled professionals who are ready to contribute to their industries from day one.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution for trade and technical education, recognized for excellence in practical training, innovation, and student success. We envision graduates who lead the way in their respective fields.

Our Story

A journey of growth and achievement since 1995

1995

Foundation

APPEC College was founded with a vision to provide practical education in trade skills.

2000

Expansion

Added Software Development and Computer Architecture programs to meet growing demand.

2005

State-of-the-Art Labs

Opened new training facilities with industry-standard equipment and technology.

2010

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with leading companies for internships and job placements.

Accreditation

Received full accreditation from the Technical Education Accreditation Board.

2000+ Alumni

Celebrated graduating over 2000 successful professionals across all four trades.
