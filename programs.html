Our Programs

Choose Your Path in One of Four Dynamic Trades

Accounting

Our Accounting program equips students with the essential skills to manage financial records, analyze data, and ensure compliance with financial regulations. You'll learn industry-standard software and gain practical experience through real-world projects that prepare you for roles in bookkeeping, auditing, and financial analysis.

Graduates are well-positioned for certifications like CPA or CMA, opening doors to careers in public accounting, corporate finance, or government agencies.

Year 1 Modules
  • Introduction to Financial Accounting
  • Business Mathematics
  • Introduction to Economics
  • Computer Applications for Business
  • Principles of Management
  • Business Communication
Year 2 Modules
  • Intermediate Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Taxation Principles
  • Auditing Fundamentals
  • Financial Management
  • Accounting Software (QuickBooks, SAP)
Tools You'll Learn
  • QuickBooks Pro
  • SAP ERP
  • Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
  • Xero Accounting
  • TurboTax
  • ACL Analytics

Software Development

Dive into the world of software development and learn to create innovative applications that solve real-world problems. Our comprehensive curriculum covers everything from foundational programming concepts to advanced development methodologies, preparing you for the fast-paced tech industry.

Work on collaborative projects, participate in hackathons, and build a portfolio that showcases your abilities to potential employers. Our industry partnerships ensure you learn the latest technologies and best practices.

Year 1 Modules
  • Introduction to Programming (Python)
  • Web Development Fundamentals (HTML/CSS)
  • Database Design & SQL
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Computer Science Fundamentals
  • Technical Writing
Year 2 Modules
  • Advanced JavaScript & Frameworks
  • React & Frontend Development
  • Backend Development (Node.js)
  • Mobile App Development
  • Software Testing & QA
  • DevOps & CI/CD
Tools You'll Learn
  • VS Code & Git
  • React & Vue.js
  • Node.js & Express
  • PostgreSQL & MongoDB
  • Docker & Kubernetes
  • Figma (UI/UX Design)

Building & Construction

Build a solid future with our comprehensive Building & Construction program. Learn essential skills in blueprint reading, carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and project management. Our state-of-the-art workshops provide hands-on training with industry-standard tools and materials.

Get certified in multiple trade areas and develop the expertise to work on residential, commercial, or industrial projects. Our program prepares you for immediate employment or entrepreneurship.

Year 1 Modules
  • Construction Fundamentals
  • Blueprint Reading & Drafting
  • Safety & OSHA Standards
  • Carpentry Basics
  • Introduction to Electrical Systems
  • Building Materials & Tools
Year 2 Modules
  • Advanced Carpentry
  • Plumbing Systems
  • Electrical Wiring & Installation
  • HVAC Fundamentals
  • Project Management
  • Green Building Practices
Tools You'll Learn
  • Power Tools (Drills, Saws, Sanders)
  • Hand Tools (Hammers, Levels, Squares)
  • Blueprint Software (AutoCAD)
  • Welding Equipment

  • Project Management Software

Computer Architecture

Master the art of computer hardware design, assembly, and maintenance. Our Computer Architecture program covers everything from circuit design to network infrastructure, preparing you for roles in IT support, system administration, and hardware engineering.

Work with cutting-edge hardware in our advanced labs, learn to troubleshoot complex systems, and develop the skills to build and maintain enterprise-level computing environments.

Year 1 Modules
  • Computer Hardware Fundamentals
  • Operating Systems (Windows & Linux)
  • Networking Basics
  • Computer Repair & Maintenance
  • Electronics Fundamentals
  • Technical Support Essentials
Year 2 Modules
  • Advanced Networking (CCNA)
  • Server Administration
  • Cloud Computing Fundamentals
  • Cybersecurity Basics
  • Virtualization Technologies
  • IoT & Embedded Systems
Tools You'll Learn
  • Multimeters & Oscilloscopes
  • Crimping & Testing Tools
  • Network Analyzers
  • VMware & VirtualBox
  • AWS & Azure Cloud
  • Cisco Packet Tracer
